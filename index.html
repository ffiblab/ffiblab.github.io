How It Works

Sound that
follows your hand.

01

Pressure, speed, and tilt

Every variable of your stroke shapes the sound in real time — press harder for a denser tone, move faster for a sharper crackle, tilt the pen and the texture shifts, just like on paper.

02

Fine-tune to your hand

Every tablet feels different. Customize the pressure curve to match yours, and dial in the tone of each tool to exactly the sound you want to hear.

03

Works everywhere, invisibly

PenSound hooks into your tablet driver at the system level — no plugins, no per-app setup. Prefer to keep it to specific apps? Add or exclude any app in one click.

04

Mute key

Hold Space to pan the canvas — PenSound stays quiet while you're not drawing, so the sound never breaks your flow.
